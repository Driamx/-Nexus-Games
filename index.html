About Nexus Games

Founded in 2015, Nexus Games started as a small indie studio with just 5 passionate developers. Our first game, "Pixel Adventures," gained a cult following and allowed us to grow into the AAA studio we are today.

We've since released 12 critically acclaimed titles across multiple platforms, winning numerous awards for innovation and storytelling.

Our team consists of over 200 talented individuals from around the world, including:

  • Game Designers
  • Programmers
  • Artists
  • Writers
  • Sound Engineers

We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workplace culture.

Our vision is to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment by:

Creating immersive worlds that players never want to leave Developing innovative gameplay mechanics Telling stories that resonate emotionally Building communities around our games
